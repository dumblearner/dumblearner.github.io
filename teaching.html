Teaching Experience

During my PhD, I was a teaching assistant in the Department of Mathematics and spent around one-fourth of my time in various teaching roles such as:

  • I have been involved with the development of the departmental question bank in STACK (System for Teaching and Assessment using a Computer algebra Kernel).
  • Conducted tutorials for first year pure math modules: Analysis 1 and 2 (MATH0003 and MATH0004), Algebra 1 and 2 (MATH0005 and MATH0006).
  • Conducted tutorials and moderator for online problem sessions for Mathematical Methods 3 (MATH0016).
  • Demonstrator for Combinatorial Optimisation (MATH0028), An Introduction to Mathematica (MATH0032), Algebra 1 (MATH0005).
  • Demonstrator for Computational Methods (MATH0058).
